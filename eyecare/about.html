About Us

Care for Eye Optometrists are one of the pioneer optical eyewear companies in India for more than 50 years. It is an independent and professional optical chain. We have more than 20 Branches in Karnataka with around 80 dedicated and qualified staff.

We always make sure to give our customers a 100% satisfied service by introducing them latest designs and models to suit individual preference.

Currently, we are one of the few local manufacturer in India for spectacles, optical lenses, spectacle cases and lens cleaning solutions
under one roof.We sell all our eyewear products both wholesale and retail nation wide with imported, branded and standard designs.

Our Vision

"Is to produce and supply high quality fashionable reasonably priced accurate spectacles and related services to our customers according
to international standards."

Our Mission

"Is to remain as the #1 Optical service supplier to the nation, and produce all types of optical goods in India for the local and to achieve-export market."
